What is Dharm

1. Saint says; Dharm is not merely to preach Dharm, it is honesty, it is one's duty for self, family, society & Country, it is to protect these , it is to do the right action which is morally, ethically correct, live & die for the country , it is to protect Dharm & protect the legacy of our ancestors.
