What is Dharm

1. Saint says; Dharm is not merely to preach Dharm, it is honesty, it is one's duty for self, family, society & Country, it is to protect these , it is to do the right action which is morally, ethically correct, live & die for the country , it is to protect Dharm & protect the legacy of our ancestors.

Shri Krishn said to Arjun

1. In Shri-Mud-Bhagwatgeeta Shri Krishn said to Arjun, " Hey Arjun! There ia nothing in this universe which I can't obtain with a mare wish. But still I do my karma ( duty). When being a GOD I am doing my duty you are just a human. You can't escape from your duty. Without your duty you won't get mukti(salvation)" .


Karma your Destiny

1. Your karmas(actions) will come back to you sooner or later in the form of results. So beware & do right karmas. This is what Shri Karshn said in Gita while giving devine knowledge to Arjun; the confused worrior.


Power of a Yogi

1. When while his raid in India; once Alaxander went to an Indian Sadhu(saint). He had heard a lot about Indian saints. He bowed in front of him & said what he can do for him. Sadhu who was sitting in Yoga pose told him to go away from there. Alaxander feeling offended said,"do you know who I am?" Sadhu without opening his eyes replied, "I know you are Alaxander & when you will go back you won't be able to take a single pin from India." Alaxander before his death said, 'If he had not been Alaxander he would have preferred to be an Indian Sadhu.'


Power of Yog

1.It is said that yogi in India used to do tapassya(preaching God in yoga pose) for more than a thousand years. I think that power is long lost with time; if I am not mistaken; because it is hardly seen now a days. But one thing is definit that they attained this power with God's blessing & power of yog combined.


Rich Culture

1. My dear Indian brothers & sisters; in this materialistic world don't forget your roots & your ancient rich cultural heritage. Our rich cultural heritage & knowledge is our strength. Yog; which you taught to the world is just one part of it.

Your roots your Country

1.You are known by your roots, your country of origin. If you abandoned your roots you are finished. Wherever you live; don't forget your roots, your country.


My country my pride

1. India is a country of great ancient sages, God, Yog and rich ancient culture. My country is my pride.


Incredible India

1. India is a country of rich ancient culture, costom, food, cloths, places & what not. India taught many things to the world including yoga. If you have not seen India; you missed something in your life.
